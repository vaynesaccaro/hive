## Stress-Test Questions

1. **Pre-mortem:** Assume this decision fails in 12 months. What was the most likely cause?
2. **Steel-man:** What is the strongest case _against_ this decision?
3. **Outside view:** What happened when others made the same call in similar contexts?
4. **Reversibility:** If we're wrong, how easily can we course-correct? What's the cost?
