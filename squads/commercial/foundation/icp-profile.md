# ICP Profile — Ideal Customer Profile

## Company
- **Size (employees):** _e.g. 50–200_
- **Revenue range:** _e.g. $1M–$10M ARR_
- **Industry:** _e.g. B2B SaaS, Professional Services_
- **Geography:** _e.g. US, LATAM_
- **Business model:** _e.g. subscription, project-based_

## Pain Points
1. _Primary pain — e.g. manual processes consuming team time_
2. _Secondary pain — e.g. lack of visibility into pipeline_
3. _Tertiary pain — e.g. difficulty scaling without headcount_

## Budget
- **Typical deal size:** _e.g. $10k–$50k/year_
- **Budget owner:** _e.g. VP Operations, CFO_
- **Budget cycle:** _e.g. annual, Q1 approval_

## Decision Maker
- **Title:** _e.g. Head of Operations, CTO_
- **Motivations:** _e.g. ROI, risk reduction, team leverage_
- **Blockers:** _e.g. procurement process, security review_

## Champions (Internal Sponsors)
- **Title:** _e.g. Manager, Senior IC_
- **Motivation:** _e.g. solves daily friction, career win_

## Disqualifiers
- _e.g. less than 10 employees_
- _e.g. no dedicated ops or tech function_
- _e.g. already locked in competing contract_
- _e.g. no budget authority at point of contact_

## Signals of Good Fit
- _e.g. recently hired ops leader_
- _e.g. growing headcount > 20% YoY_
- _e.g. posted job for role our product replaces_
