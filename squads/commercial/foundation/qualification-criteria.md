# Qualification Criteria

## Framework: BANT + MEDDIC Hybrid

Fill out during or immediately after discovery call.

---

## BANT

| Criteria | Question to Ask | Status |
|----------|----------------|--------|
| **Budget** | Do they have allocated budget? What is the range? | ☐ Confirmed / ☐ Estimated / ☐ Unknown |
| **Authority** | Is the contact the decision maker? Who else signs? | ☐ DM / ☐ Champion / ☐ Gatekeeper |
| **Need** | Is the pain explicit and urgent? | ☐ Explicit / ☐ Latent / ☐ None |
| **Timeline** | When do they want to go live? Is there a forcing event? | ☐ < 30d / ☐ 30–90d / ☐ > 90d / ☐ Unknown |

---

## MEDDIC (for complex / enterprise deals)

| Criteria | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| **Metrics** — quantified business impact | _e.g. save 10h/week, reduce churn by 5%_ |
| **Economic Buyer** — who controls budget | _Name + title:_ |
| **Decision Criteria** — what matters to them | _e.g. security, integrations, ease of use_ |
| **Decision Process** — steps to sign | _e.g. legal review → CFO → sign_ |
| **Identify Pain** — confirmed root cause | _Primary pain:_ |
| **Champion** — internal advocate | _Name + title:_ |

---

## Scoring Rubric

| Score | Criteria Met | Action |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| **3–4 BANT** | Full qualification confirmed | Move to Proposal |
| **2 BANT** | Partial — missing budget or timeline | Nurture, revisit in 30d |
| **0–1 BANT** | Poor fit or too early | Mark Unqualified / Recycle |

---

## Disqualify If
- No budget in current cycle and no approval path
- No decision maker reachable within 2 follow-ups
- Timeline > 12 months with no forcing event
- Pain doesn't match core product value prop
