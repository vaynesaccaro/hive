# Pipeline Playbook

## Stages Overview
```
Lead → Qualified → Proposal → Negotiation → Closed (Won / Lost)
```

---

## Stage Definitions

### 1. Lead
- **Entry:** Contact captured (inbound form, outreach, referral)
- **Exit:** Confirmed fit with ICP + decision maker identified
- **SLA:** First touch within **24h**; qualification call within **5 business days**
- **Owner:** SDR / AE

### 2. Qualified
- **Entry:** BANT/MEDDIC criteria met (see qualification-criteria.md)
- **Exit:** Discovery call completed, pain confirmed, next step agreed
- **SLA:** Discovery call within **7 business days** of qualification
- **Owner:** AE

### 3. Proposal
- **Entry:** Scope and pricing drafted; stakeholders aligned
- **Exit:** Proposal delivered and acknowledged
- **SLA:** Proposal sent within **5 business days** of discovery
- **Owner:** AE + Solutions

### 4. Negotiation
- **Entry:** Prospect reviewing proposal; objections raised
- **Exit:** Terms agreed verbally or in writing
- **SLA:** Follow-up within **48h** of each prospect interaction
- **Owner:** AE + Leadership (if discount > threshold)

### 5. Closed
- **Won:** Contract signed, kickoff scheduled
- **Lost:** Reason logged (price / timing / competitor / no decision)
- **SLA:** Contract sent within **24h** of verbal close
- **Owner:** AE → handoff to CS

---

## Escalation Rules
- Deal stalled > **14 days** in any stage → flag to manager
- Discount > **20%** → VP approval required
- Enterprise deal (>$_X_) → exec sponsor assigned
