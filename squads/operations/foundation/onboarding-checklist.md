# New Team Member Onboarding Checklist

**Name:** ___  **Role:** ___  **Start date:** ___  **Manager:** ___

---

## Before Day 1 (Manager / Ops)
- [ ] Equipment ordered and configured
- [ ] Accounts created: email, Slack, Linear, 1Password, GitHub, etc.
- [ ] First day agenda sent
- [ ] Buddy / onboarding partner assigned
- [ ] Welcome message sent to team

---

## Day 1

### Admin
- [ ] Equipment received and working
- [ ] All access confirmed (email, Slack, tools)
- [ ] 1Password vault access set up
- [ ] Employment docs signed (if not done)

### People
- [ ] 1:1 with manager (30–60 min) — context, expectations, first 30 days
- [ ] Meet the team (async intro post or sync call)
- [ ] Introduction to buddy / onboarding partner

### Orientation
- [ ] Company mission, values, and current strategy reviewed
- [ ] Org structure explained
- [ ] Comms norms: async tools, response expectations, meeting culture

---

## Week 1
- [ ] Read key foundation docs for squad (CLAUDE.md, STATE.md, foundation/)
- [ ] Shadow at least 2 existing team workflows
- [ ] Attend first team standup / weekly sync
- [ ] Complete security onboarding (password manager, 2FA everywhere)
- [ ] 1:1 with manager: day 3 check-in
- [ ] Identify first small deliverable — quick win for week 2

---

## Month 1
- [ ] First deliverable shipped
- [ ] 1:1 rhythm established with manager
- [ ] Met key cross-functional partners (at least 3)
- [ ] 30-day retrospective: what's clear, what's confusing, what's missing
- [ ] OKRs or goals for first 90 days agreed

---

## Month 1 Success Criteria
- New hire can describe what the team does and how their role contributes
- New hire has shipped at least one meaningful piece of work independently
- New hire knows who to ask for what
