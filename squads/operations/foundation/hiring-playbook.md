# Hiring Playbook

---

## Process Stages

```
Job Spec → Sourcing → Screen → Technical/Skill Assessment → Interview Loop → Reference Check → Offer → Close
```

| Stage | Owner | SLA |
|-------|-------|-----|
| Job spec approved | Hiring Manager | Before opening req |
| First screen | Recruiter / Ops | Within 5 business days of apply |
| Assessment sent | Recruiter | Within 2 business days of screen |
| Interview loop | Panel | Within 7 business days of assessment |
| Reference check | Recruiter | Within 2 business days of final interview |
| Offer out | Ops / Founder | Within 2 business days of decision |

---

## Scorecard Template

**Role:** ___  **Level:** ___  **Opening date:** ___

| Criteria | Weight | Must-Have? | Notes |
|----------|--------|-----------|-------|
| _e.g. Core skill X_ | High | ✅ Yes | _e.g. 3+ years hands-on_ |
| _e.g. Communication_ | Medium | ✅ Yes | _Async-first environment_ |
| _e.g. Domain knowledge_ | Low | ❌ Nice to have | _Can be learned_ |

**Interview rating per candidate:** 1 (no hire) / 2 (lean no) / 3 (lean yes) / 4 (strong hire)

## Offer Checklist
- [ ] Scorecard reviewed — clear hire decision
- [ ] Reference check completed (at least 2 references)
- [ ] Compensation within band for level
- [ ] Equity/benefits approved by founder if above standard
- [ ] Offer letter generated from template
- [ ] Verbal offer extended before sending letter
- [ ] Follow-up call scheduled if no response in 48h

---

## Decline Protocol
- Notify declined candidates within 5 business days
- Provide brief, honest reason if requested (for final-round candidates)
- Keep strong near-miss candidates in talent pool with consent
