# Stranger Test Protocol

A "stranger test" checks whether a process, product, or document is understandable and usable by someone with no prior context — simulating a new user, customer, or team member encountering it cold.

---

## When to Run
- After writing a new SOP, onboarding doc, or product flow
- Before launching a new feature to a broader audience
- When internal feedback says "this is obvious" but users are confused
- Quarterly audit of core customer-facing flows

---

## Preparation

- [ ] Define the scope: what exactly is being tested?
- [ ] Define the goal: what should the stranger be able to accomplish?
- [ ] Identify the tester: someone who has **zero context** on this specific process or feature
  - Valid: new hire, someone from a different squad, external contractor
  - Invalid: person who helped build it, or was briefed on it beforehand
- [ ] Prepare the materials as-is — do NOT clean up or explain verbally before the test
- [ ] Prepare observation method: screen share, written report, or structured debrief

---

## Test Conditions

- Tester receives only what a real user would receive (the doc, the screen, the flow)
- No hints, corrections, or context from the observer during the test
- Observer notes: where the tester pauses, asks questions, or makes wrong assumptions
- Time limit: _e.g. 15 minutes for a single flow; 30 min for a full process doc_

---

## Pass / Fail Criteria

| Criterion | Pass | Fail |
|-----------|------|------|
| Completes primary goal without help | ✅ | ❌ |
| No critical steps missed or misunderstood | ✅ | ❌ |
| No questions about meaning of terms used | ✅ | ❌ |
| Estimated effort matches actual time | ✅ within 20% | ❌ off by > 50% |

---

## Debrief

After the test, ask:
1. Where did you hesitate or feel unsure?
2. Was anything missing that you expected to find?
3. What would you change about how this was presented?

---

## Output
- List of friction points with location (step, section, screen)
- Severity: blocking / slowing / cosmetic
- Owner assigned to each fix
- Retest date if blocking issues found

---

## Retest
- Required if any **blocking** finding
- Optional if only cosmetic/minor
- Goal: retest within **10 business days** of fix
