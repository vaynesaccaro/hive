# Content Calendar

## Active Channels

| Channel | Frequency | Primary Content Type | Goal |
|---------|-----------|---------------------|------|
| _e.g. LinkedIn_ | _3x/week_ | _Thought leadership, case studies_ | _Brand authority_ |
| _e.g. Email newsletter_ | _Weekly_ | _Curated insights + product updates_ | _Nurture / retention_ |
| _e.g. Blog_ | _2x/month_ | _SEO articles, how-tos_ | _Organic traffic_ |
| _e.g. Instagram_ | _4x/week_ | _Short-form, behind the scenes_ | _Audience growth_ |
| _e.g. YouTube_ | _Monthly_ | _Deep-dive tutorials_ | _Product education_ |

---

## Content Types per Channel

### LinkedIn
- Original opinion posts
- Customer stories / results
- Team / culture moments
- Product feature highlights

### Email
- Weekly roundup (3 bullets)
- Monthly deep-dive
- Triggered campaigns (onboarding, renewal)

### Blog
- SEO long-form (1500–2500 words)
- Product use case walkthroughs
- Comparison / competitive

---

## Monthly Theme Structure

| Week | Theme Focus |
|------|-------------|
| Week 1 | _e.g. Industry trend / problem framing_ |
| Week 2 | _e.g. Product / solution angle_ |
| Week 3 | _e.g. Customer story / social proof_ |
| Week 4 | _e.g. Educational / how-to_ |

---

## Approval Workflow
1. Draft → **Content Lead** review (48h SLA)
2. Legal/sensitive topics → **Head of Marketing** approval
3. Scheduled in tool: _e.g. Buffer, Hootsuite, Notion_

---

## Review Cadence
- Weekly: next-week content queue confirmed
- Monthly: performance review + theme planning
