# Brand Voice

## Personality
_Describe the brand in 3–5 adjectives:_
- _e.g. Direct, Knowledgeable, Pragmatic, Ambitious, Approachable_

## Tone
- **Default tone:** _e.g. Professional but conversational — not corporate, not casual_
- **On social media:** _e.g. More direct, shorter sentences, opinion-forward_
- **On sales/landing pages:** _e.g. Benefit-led, urgency without hype_
- **On support/docs:** _e.g. Clear, patient, step-by-step_

---

## Vocabulary

### Use
- _e.g. "build", "ship", "results", "straightforward", "you"_
- _e.g. Specific numbers when available_
- _e.g. Active voice_

### Avoid
- _e.g. "leverage", "synergy", "game-changing", "revolutionary"_
- _e.g. Passive voice_
- _e.g. Jargon without explanation_
- _e.g. Hyperbole ("best", "fastest", "only")_

---

## Writing Examples

### Good
> _"We help ops teams cut manual work by half — without hiring."_

### Bad
> _"Our cutting-edge, best-in-class platform leverages AI to synergize your workflow."_

---

## Brand Personality Anchors
| Dimension | We Are | We Are Not |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Style | Direct | Blunt / Rude |
| Expertise | Knowledgeable | Condescending |
| Energy | Confident | Arrogant |
| Accessibility | Approachable | Informal |
